A Love Letter To G*59


The love I have for the G*59 Record Label is like no other. Never have I found a singular place where all my favorite artists reside.

The artists of this record label speak words and explore subjects of suicidal ideation and depression, themes not widely expressed in the music industry.

setting themselves apart from other mainstream artists. This underground label has attracted a massive cult following, gaining more and more popularity as the years have gone on.

Like so many others, I love music. It has the power to influence how you feel, think, and act.

Everybody goes through pain in their lives. Some cope by meditating, others by journaling. I like to cope by listening to music

Being able to listen to these artists have helped me deal with pain and consoled me when I needed it most.

The first artists of G*59 that I listened to were those of $uicideBoy$. No, I never felt depressed or thoughts of suicide,

but I suffered from emotional suppression. I bottled up my feelings inside telling no one, and let it eat me from the inside.
My one wish is that I discovered this record label sooner. G*59, from the bottom of my heart, THANK YOU for being there for me.
